Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish combining spicy chickpea curry (chole) with deep-fried, fluffy bread (bhature). It's rich, flavorful, and often enjoyed as a heavy breakfast or festive meal.

Chole is cooked with onions, tomatoes, and aromatic spices, while bhature is made from a soft, fermented dough. The crispy exterior and soft center of bhature perfectly complement the tangy, masaledar chole.

Ingredients

  • For Chole: Chickpeas,onion,tomato,garlic,cumin,coriander,turmeric,garam masala, chilli
  • For Bhature: All-purpose flour,curd,baking soda/powder,salt,oil,water

Steps

  • Soak and cook chickpeas: Soak overnight, pressure-cook with tea bag (optional) until soft.
  • Make Chole: Saut√© onion, garlic, ginger; add tomatoes and spices. Add chickpeas and simmer.
  • Prepare bhature dough: Mix flour, curd, baking soda, salt; knead into a soft dough. Rest 2+ hrs.
  • Roll and fry bhature: Roll into discs and deep fry until puffed and golden.
  • Serve hot: With chole, sliced onions, and lemon.
